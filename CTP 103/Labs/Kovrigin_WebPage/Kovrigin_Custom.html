How to take good care of your skin

The benefits of washing your face every night

  1. Removes make-up
  2. Removes oils and pollutans
  3. Keeps skin looking younger
  4. Helps cell turn over
  5. Helps skincare absorption

Protecting your skin from the sun

  • Seek shade, especially between 10 AM and 4 PM
  • Don't get sunburned
  • Avoid tanning and never use tanning beds
  • Use broad spectrum (UVA/UVB) sunscreen

Source:Skin Cancer Foundation
